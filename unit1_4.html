1.4

Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

There are many reasons, why a digital project is at a risk to fail. There could be a software update in which the software you’re using for the project is not compatible with the current up-to-date software. Competitors who the same tactics for your digital project, in which the project/product loses potential customers because the competitors used it first. Team Members departing or taken absent or bad project management such as missing deadlines. [8][9]
